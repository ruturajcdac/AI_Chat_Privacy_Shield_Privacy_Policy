Privacy Policy — AI Chat Privacy Shield

Last updated: 01/03/2026

AI Chat Privacy Shield does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or conversation content on external servers.

What the extension does

It applies a visual CSS blur to the chat history sidebar on supported AI platforms. All blur preferences are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local and never leave your machine.

Data we access

The extension reads the structure of the webpage's sidebar DOM solely to apply visual styling. It does not read, log, or transmit any chat content or titles.

Permissions used

storage: to save your blur preference locally.
activeTab: to apply visual changes to the active tab.
